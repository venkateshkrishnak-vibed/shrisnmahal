About Shri SN Mahal

A luxurious marriage hall in the heart of Trichy. Nestled within a picturesque 1.5-acre campus, our luxurious venue offers an enchanting ambiance for your special day. With abundant car parking facilities and a fully air-conditioned hall and dining area on the ground floor, your guests will enjoy utmost comfort.

Our cozy air-conditioned rooms and modern interiors add an extra touch of elegance to your celebration. Conveniently located just 5 minutes away from the revered Murugan Temple, and a mere 15 minutes from both Trichy Central Bus Stand and Trichy Railway Junction, our venue ensures easy accessibility for all. Make cherished memories at our splendid wedding destination.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our venue

What is the capacity of the main hall?

Our venue can accommodate up to 1000 guests comfortably, with 750 seating and 1000 floating capacity to suit different event types.

What is the capacity of dining hall?

Our AC dining hall can comfortably accommodate 200 guests at a time.

Do you take corporate bookings for meetings/award ceremonies?

Yes, we also regularly host award ceremonies, zonal meetings and other corporate events for corporates in Trichy, Chennai, Bangalore, Coimbatore, Salem, Madurai etc.

What all events do you host?

We host a variety of events including weddings, receptions, engagement, baby-shower, birthday parties, corporate meetings, and cultural programs. Our versatile space can be adapted to suit your specific needs.

How many rooms do you have?

We have a total of 15 rooms and all are fully air-conditioned.

Do you provide catering services?

Yes, we have partnered with a caterer who specialises in South Indian, North Indian, and Chinese cuisines.

Is parking available?

Yes, we have ample parking space for over 100 cars and 300 bikes.

Can we customize the decoration?

Absolutely! We offer professional decorators with 15+ years of service but you can of course have your own decorators and customize the venue according to your theme and preferences without causing any damage to the venue.
